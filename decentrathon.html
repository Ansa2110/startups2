Decentrathon

THE LARGEST WEB3 HACKATHON IN CENTRAL ASIA

About Decentrathon

  • Decentrathon - 'decentralized hackathon'.
  • Decentrathon will be held simultaneously in multiple locations across Kazakhstan in non-stop regime

Requirments

  • Participation is completely free
  • You can participate both solo and as a team
  • Team captain must be over 18 years old
  • No more than 4 people per one team

The first multivenue hackathon

  • 9 regions
  • 15 locations
  • 72 hourse

Prize fund

  • Prize for 1st place: 5000 USD
  • Prize for 2nd place: 3500 USD
  • Prize for 3rd place: 1500 USD

FAQ

What is hackathon?

It is a competition for IT enthusiasts where you have to solve certain technological problem in a short time and non-stop.

What if i dont have a team?

You can find teammates in this chat, or you can participate in solo.

When the case will be released?

All the details for the case will be given on the opening ceremony of Decentrathon.

Do i need to be a student of university to participate?

No, you don't have to be a student. Everyone is welcome to participate.

Can i participate online?

Yes, you can. But one of team members must be offline on the first day on 1 of any 15 locations across the Kazakhstan where the hackathon will be held simultaneously.

What about meals?

There will be one-time meals provided during every day of the hackathon.
