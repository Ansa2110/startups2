Jetisu Startup Challenge

Startup competition for founders from the Zhetisu region who create solutions in AgriTech, TravelTech, Creative Industries areas.

About Decentrathon

Jetisu Startup Challenge is a startup competition for founders from the Jetisu region who create solutions in AgriTech, TravelTech, Creative Industries areas.

If you are creating a startup in these areas and looking for where you can show your talent, find like-minded people and get feedback from experts, professionals from these areas, welcome to the Jetisu Startup Challenge!

The winners will receive valuable experience, cash prizes and media exposure!

Requirments

  • Participation is completely free
  • You can participate both solo and as a team
  • Team captain must be over 18 years old
  • No more than 4 people per one team

The first multivenue hackathon

  • 9 regions
  • 15 locations
  • 72 hourse

Prize fund

  • The total prize fund is 1 million tenge.
  • The main prizes are valuable experience of participating in the competition, new acquaintances and skills.

FAQ

For whom?

Jetisu Startup Challenge is held for talented founders of AgriTech, TravelTech, Creative Industry startups who will be able to apply their solutions in the Jetisu area.

What is the purpose?

Find and support talents, promising projects. To give start-upers from the regions an opportunity to show themselves.

How does it go?

The competition will be in a hybrid format. The main part of the program will be held online, and the winners will be announced offline in Taldykorgan.
