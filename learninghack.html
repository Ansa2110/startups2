Learning Hack 2023

The second central Asian hackathon on educational product development and
the first meetup "HR 3.0 accelerated team and business development".

About Learning Hack 2023

LEARNING HACK is a competition for those who develop in the creation of training programs, are looking for new solutions in training and are ready to prove themselves in a team competition.

Tasks are provided by customer companies, and the jury is experienced experts and professionals in the field of education.

This is the second face-to-face hackathon for the development of educational products in Central Asia.

LEARNING HACK last year brought together everyone who develops in the creation of training programs in Kazakhstan: 15 teams of participants, 5 case customers and 19 companies in the hackathon jury.

This year the competition is open to representatives from all over Central Asia.

The working language is Russian.

What participation in the hackathon gives?

  • The best learning development tools from the experts
  • Networking with international market specialists, useful materials and expert sessions during the competition
  • Opportunity to share your idea and exchange experience in the professional community
  • Acquaintance with real Customers and the needs of companies in training. The best solutions Customers can hire or invite a hackathon team to implement it
  • Experience in public speaking and project defense
  • Development of project management skills through solving real business problems
  • Certificates and prizes from Customers and Partners

Who can participate?

  • Methodologists and trainers
  • Product and project managers in the field of training and development, producers of online courses
  • HR, T&D and employer brand specialists
  • Teachers and lecturers who are interested in the methodology

You can apply to participate in the hackathon with a team, including a corporate team (3-5 people) or individually (in this case, we will help you create a team right at the hackathon).

Only adult participants are allowed to participate in the Hackathon.

What cases to be solved?

Cases will be provided by Customers - representatives of business (companies) who are looking for solutions to their tasks of training and developing employees

Each team will be able to choose a case to solve, but within the established limits (each case is solved by 2-3 teams)

Case example: develop a program aimed at motivating, retaining and developing high-potential employees of various positions and departments (specialists, managers, 50/50 ratio, 70 people) for further promotion within the company

How will the evaluation be carried out?

Ideas will be evaluated by an expert jury according to pre-formulated criteria, you will learn more about them at a briefing for participants before the start of the hackathon. The task of the team is to prepare a presentation with their proposal and present it to the jury within the allotted time.

Details about what awaits you on each day of the hackathon and recommendations for preparation will be sent to your email after registration.

FAQ

Can I participate alone?

The hackathon format involves participation by teams. If you do not have your own team, we will help you to select one from among other individual participants.

Do I need to prepare any materials in advance??

No, this is not necessary. You will learn about the case on the opening day of the Hackathon - June 2, and you will have enough time to prepare a presentation of your concept at the event itself.

When will we learn about case topics?

Cases will be presented by Customers on the opening day of the Hackathon - June 2. You will have the opportunity to get to know your team and choose a topic you would like to work on. Important: the number of places per case is limited!
