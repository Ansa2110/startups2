JAS AI: AI-Powered Video Analytics

Suggest your idea in the field of video analytics and implement it using computer vision technologies.

Why should you participate

  • Get a job offer
  • Opportunity to receive grant and investment support for product development
  • Invaluable experience for personal and professional growth
  • An opportunity to work and communicate with professionals in your field. Find like-minded people
  • Win a cash prize with a total prize pool of 2,000,000 KZT
  • Become a member of the JAS Ventures community

Who can participate

Individual participants or teams up to 5 people
Age 16+

For whom

Middle level developers and above with knowledge of:

  • Python
  • JavaScript
  • Golang
  • Svelte
  • Meteor
  • React

Preferably with experience in developing or adapting computer vision algorithms

Prize fund

The prize fund of the hackathon is 2,000,000 tenge, where:

  • Prize for 1st place: 1,000,000 tenge;
  • Prize for 2nd place: 500,000 tenge;
  • Two prizes for 3rd place: 250,000 tenge each.

As well as the opportunity to get a job in a company that is a sponsor of the hackathon.

Agenda

April 7, 12:00 - Preparatory session, distribution of tasks online and offline, with access to offline sites in Astana, Almaty;

April 7, 14:00 - April 9, 13:00 - 48-hour marathon, work on the task, with access to an offline platform in Astana;

April 9, 14:00 - Presentation of the participants to the jury online and offline, with access to the offline platform in Astana;

April 10, 13:00 - Announcement of winners online and offline, with access to offline sites in Astana, Almaty.

Organizers

The sponsor of the hackathon is a company from the United States of America, a full-cycle system integrator specializing in the supply of solutions in the field of smart cities, artificial intelligence, information security and big data.

JAS Ventures Limited and JAS Ventures Almaty are a young venture and investment ecosystem whose main activity is to proactively search for startups through networking and various incubation and acceleration programs in Kazakhstan and beyond, with subsequent funding of promising, scalable and fast-growing startups.
