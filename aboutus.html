About Us

Learn more about Startups.kz, our mission, and our vision for the future of the startup ecosystem in Kazakhstan.

Our Mission

Startups.kz is a comprehensive platform for promoting and fostering the growth of the startup ecosystem in Kazakhstan. We aim to provide entrepreneurs, investors, and enthusiasts with up-to-date information on startup events, workshops, and conferences happening all around the country.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a thriving startup community in Kazakhstan that encourages innovation, collaboration, and growth. We aspire to become a go-to resource for all stakeholders in the startup ecosystem, connecting people, ideas, and opportunities to drive positive change and economic development in our country.
